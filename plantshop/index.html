Indoor plants

Thriving in low light, indoor plants like snake plants and peace lilies purify the air and add greenery to your home or office.

Outdoor plants

Best suited for gardens and patios, outdoor plants like roses and sunflowers thrive in sunlight and create vibrant, natural landscapes.

Medical plants

Used for natural healing, medicinal plants like aloe vera and peppermint offer benefits like soothing, anti-inflammatory, and relaxing properties.
